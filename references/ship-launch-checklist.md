# Ship Launch Checklist

Use alongside `aw-ship`.

## Launch Readiness

- required test and review evidence exists
- deploy evidence exists or the blocker is explicit
- rollback path is documented
- known risks are named

## Rollout

- monitoring or health checks are identified
- smoke checks are identified
- ownership for follow-through is clear

## GHL Notes

- staging flows should record build links, versioned entry or service evidence, and smoke outcome
- frontend releases should note versioned remote-entry or route proof when applicable
- service and worker releases should note health or workload status when available

## Closeout

- final outcome is explicit
- follow-up work is named
- release artifact is updated with launch or blocker notes
